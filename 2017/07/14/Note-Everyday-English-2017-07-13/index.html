[Note] Everyday English - 13th July

2017-07-14
English
English

Pronunciation

  1. Genre: /Ʒɑ:nrə/
  2. Lyric: /lɪrɪk/

Words & Expressions

  1. along with it
    • 그것과 동시에, 함께
  2. get along with someone
    • ~와 잘 어울리다.
  3. all-time favorite
    • best

Sentences

  1. I couldn’t understand their lyrics.
  2. It reminds me of the girl I used to like.

etc

  1. wind down
    • 긴장을 풀다.
    • e.g. It never fails to help them wind down.
  2. be in the mood for
    • ~을 원하다, ~할 강한 욕구를 가지다.
    • e.g. What are you in the mood for?
  3. lift one’s spirits
    • ~을 행복하게 하다.
    • e.g. Nothing lifts my spirits like shopping.
  4. push one’s limits
    • 최선을 다하다.
    • It can inspire you to push your limits when exercising.
  5. bland
    • 재미없는, 건조한
    • Without the soundtrack to go along with music, the movies is a bit bland.
